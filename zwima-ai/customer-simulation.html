Zwima AI Revenue Simulation

Internal customer scenario model for plan mix, monthly revenue, monthly cost, gross profit, and annual totals.

Revenue simulation for the first growth stages of Zwima AI. The model uses Starter, Business, and Pro plan mixes with estimated monthly cost per plan to show gross profit potential.

Plans

Starter

49€ / month

Entry plan for pilots, first API usage, small internal workflows, and low-risk trial conversions.

Pro

199€ / month

Core growth plan for teams using AI in active operations, internal tools, or client-facing workflows.

Business

499€ / month

Higher-volume plan for companies with multiple users, heavier API usage, and stronger support needs.
