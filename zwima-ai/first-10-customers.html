4. Demo Flow

  1. Introduction: Confirm the prospect's role, current AI usage, and the business problem they want to solve.
  2. AI Models Overview: Explain available models, common use cases, and how Zwima simplifies access and cost control.
  3. Dashboard Demo: Show account setup, usage overview, API access, credits, billing, and key workflow screens.
  4. Pricing: Walk through Starter, Pro, Business, and trial options based on expected usage.
  5. Trial Access: Agree on trial goal, owner, start date, success criteria, and next check-in.
