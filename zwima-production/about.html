About Zwima AI

AI Workforce for European SMEs.

Zwima AI is AI Workforce for European SMEs from Zwima Technologie GmbH. We help SMEs replace repetitive office work with AI employees for sales, marketing, customer service, and operations.

Our Positioning

AI employees for practical business teams.

European SMEs often know that AI can help, but they need concrete productivity gains instead of disconnected tools. Zwima AI turns repetitive office work into structured AI workflows that teams can use every day.

Our focus is clean implementation, clear communication, and support for realistic business workflows such as lead generation, proposal drafting, support replies, content production, reporting, and internal process automation.
