FAQ

Common questions.

What is an AI employee?

An AI employee is a workflow assistant configured for repetitive business tasks such as lead research, sales emails, support replies, content creation, or reporting.

Which companies is Zwima AI built for?

Zwima AI is built for European SMEs with 5-200 employees, especially owner-led companies in trading, industrial services, consulting, B2B e-commerce, agencies, and export.

Do we need a large IT project to start?

No. Zwima AI is designed for practical adoption without long enterprise procurement or a complex internal AI program.

Is customer data used for model training?

No. Customer data is not used by Zwima AI to train AI models.
